# Your License Number

Your state-issued professional licence number is how StaffPick confirms you're licensed to practise. It's entered in **Step 5 (Credentials)** of your profile, on the licence document.

## Why it's required

StaffPick verifies clinician licences so the scheduling team can place you with confidence. Depending on your discipline, that verification is either:

- **Automated** — for disciplines with a live licensing API (e.g. Physical Therapy), StaffPick checks your licence number against the state board and marks it verified automatically.
- **Deep-link lookup** — for disciplines whose board only offers a search page (e.g. OT and SLP via the Florida MQA), the scheduling team opens a pre-filled lookup using your number and confirms the result.

Either way, **the number has to be on file**. Without it, your licence falls back to a manual review, which is slower.

## Where to find your number

Your licence number is printed on your state licence and is searchable on your state board's verification site. For Florida it's on your **DOH/MQA** licence. Examples of the format:

- **PT** — e.g. `PT34980`
- **OT** — e.g. `OT12345`
- **SLP** — e.g. `SP0001234`

Enter it exactly as it appears on the licence, including any letter prefix.

## What happens during verification

1. You enter the number in Step 5 and upload the licence document.
2. After you submit, the scheduling team runs verification (automatically or via the board's lookup page).
3. Your credential is marked **verified**, **pending manual confirmation**, or **failed**, and you're contactable if anything needs correcting.

## Keep it current

When you renew, update the credential and expiry date. A lapsed licence can remove you from the matching pool until it's refreshed.
