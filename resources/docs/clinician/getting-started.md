# Getting Started

StaffPick connects you with patient cases that fit your discipline, your location, and your availability — without the back-and-forth of phone tag. This guide explains how the platform works from your side.

## How it works for you

1. **You build a profile** once — who you are, your discipline and specialties, where and how far you'll travel, when you're available, and your credentials. This is what the matching engine uses to find you the right cases.
2. **We match you to cases.** When a referral comes in that fits your profile, our matching engine ranks you against other clinicians on distance, language, specialties, availability and more.
3. **You get offered cases one at a time.** If you're the best fit, you're offered first. You'll see enough to decide — discipline, general area, schedule, rate — and you can accept or decline.
4. **You accept what works.** The first clinician to accept gets the case. Once you accept, the scheduling team takes it from there.

## What you control

- **Your service area** — a radius and an optional drawn zone that decide which cases are even close enough to reach you.
- **Your availability** — the days and times you can take work.
- **Your specialties and languages** — these make you a stronger match for the right cases.
- **Your responses** — accept the cases that fit, decline the ones that don't (a quick reason helps us match you better next time).

## First steps

1. **[Complete your profile](completing-your-profile)** — the six-step wizard. You can't be matched until it's submitted and approved.
2. Make sure your **[license number](your-license-number)** is on file so your credentials can be verified.
3. Learn how **[offers work](responding-to-offers)** so you're ready when the first one arrives.

> A **?** icon on most screens opens help for exactly what you're doing.
