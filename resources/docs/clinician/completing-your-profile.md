# Completing Your Profile

Your profile is built through a **six-step wizard**. Your progress saves automatically as you go, so you can stop and come back without losing anything. You can't be matched to cases until the profile is submitted and approved by the scheduling team.

## Step 1 — Personal Information

Your name, contact details, and home/base address. The address is **geocoded** (placed on a map) so the matching engine knows where you are. After typing your address, use **Find address on map** to drop the pin; if it lands in the wrong spot, drag it to your exact location. Your position has to be set for matching to place you.

## Step 2 — Professional

Your **discipline** (PT, OT, or SLP), your **specialties**, the **languages** you speak with patients, and your years of experience. Specialties and languages both feed the matching score — the more accurately you fill these in, the better your matches. Choose the discipline first; the specialty list filters to match it.

## Step 3 — Service Area

How far you'll travel (your **preferred** and **maximum** radius) and, optionally, a **drawn service zone**. The radius is the simple version; the zone lets you carve out the exact area you'll cover. See **[Service Area & Zones](service-area-zones)** for detail.

## Step 4 — Availability

The days and time windows you can take appointments. Add a window per block of availability. Cases whose schedule fits your windows score higher for you.

## Step 5 — Credentials

Upload your required documents — state licence, CPR, liability insurance, W-9, background check. For licence types, enter your **licence number** so it can be verified automatically. See **[Your License Number](your-license-number)**.

## Step 6 — Review & Submit

A summary of everything you've entered. Check it over and **submit for review**. The scheduling team approves your profile, after which you become eligible for matching. You'll be notified when you're approved (or if something needs fixing).

## After you submit

- Your status moves to **pending review**. You can still edit and re-submit.
- Once approved, you're **active** and in the matching pool.
- Keep your availability and credentials current — expired credentials can pull you out of matching.
