# Responding to Offers

When you're the best fit for a case, StaffPick offers it to you — and only you — for a set window before moving on. Responding promptly keeps you first in line.

## Finding your offers

Open **My Offers** from your dashboard. It lists every case currently offered to you, each with a countdown to when the offer expires. Offers are sent through your preferred channel (portal, email, or SMS); whichever way you hear about it, you respond in **My Offers**.

## What's in an offer

An offer gives you what you need to decide — **without** any protected health information:

- the **discipline** and any required specialties,
- the **general area** and distance,
- the **schedule / frequency**,
- the **rate**.

You won't see the patient's name or details until you've accepted and the case is assigned to you.

## Accepting

Click **Accept**. The first clinician to accept gets the case — so if it fits, don't wait. On acceptance:

- the case becomes yours and moves to **Assigned Pending**,
- any competing offers for that case are withdrawn,
- the scheduling team picks it up to activate and share the full details.

## Declining

Click **Decline** and choose a **reason** (e.g. *Out of range*, *Schedule full*, *Not my specialty*, *Unavailable*). The reason isn't busywork — it helps us match you better next time, and it lets the case move on to the next clinician immediately rather than waiting for the window to expire.

## If an offer expires

If you don't respond before the countdown ends, the offer expires and the case is offered to the next clinician automatically. You haven't done anything wrong — but responding before expiry keeps you in the running and keeps your response record strong.

## Tips

- Turn on notifications for your preferred channel so you catch offers quickly.
- A quick decline is better than a silent timeout — both pass the case along, but a reason improves your future matches.
