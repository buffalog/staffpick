# Getting Started

First Class Therapy Solutions uses StaffPick to take in your patient referrals and match them with the right therapist quickly. This short guide explains how to submit a referral and what happens next.

## What StaffPick does for you

When you send a referral, StaffPick:

1. **Records it** with a unique reference number you can quote any time you follow up.
2. **Matches it** to the best-fit therapist by discipline, location, language and availability.
3. **Places it** — a therapist accepts the case and FCTS coordinates the rest.

You don't manage any of the matching — you submit the referral, and FCTS takes it from there. You'll have a reference number to track it.

## How to submit a referral

Use the secure intake link FCTS gave your office. It opens a simple form where you provide the patient and case details. There's nothing to install and no login — just the link.

- **[Submitting a Referral](submitting-a-referral)** walks through the form field by field.
- **[After You Submit](after-you-submit)** explains what happens once it's in.
- **[Common Questions](common-questions)** answers the things referring offices ask most.

## Before you start

Have these handy to make the form quick:

- the patient's name and service address,
- the **discipline** needed (Physical, Occupational, or Speech therapy),
- any therapy authorization details,
- any preferences (e.g. language, provider gender).

> Your intake link is unique to your office — it tags every referral as coming from you. Keep it private.
