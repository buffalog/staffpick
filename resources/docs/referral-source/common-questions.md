# Common Questions

Answers to the questions referring offices ask most.

## How do I submit a referral?

Use the secure intake link FCTS provided to your office. It opens a short form — no login required. See **[Submitting a Referral](submitting-a-referral)**.

## Do I need an account or password?

No. Your intake link is unique to your office and stands in for a login. Keep it private, since every referral submitted through it is recorded as coming from you.

## How long until a therapist is assigned?

It varies with the discipline and how much therapist coverage there is in the patient's area. Many cases are matched and offered quickly; cases in thinner-coverage areas can take longer while FCTS widens the search. You'll have a reference number to check on it any time.

## How do I check on a referral I already sent?

Contact FCTS and quote the **reference number** (`R-XXXXXX`) from your confirmation screen. That's the quickest way to pull up your exact case.

## What if I picked the wrong discipline or made a mistake?

Contact FCTS with your reference number and they'll correct it. Discipline and address matter most — they determine which therapists can be matched — so flag those quickly.

## Can I request a specific language or provider gender?

Yes. The form lets you note a **preferred language** and a **provider gender preference**. Use them only when they genuinely matter to the patient, because each preference narrows the pool of therapists who can take the case.

## What information do you need about the patient?

At minimum the patient's name, service address, and the discipline required. Authorization details, frequency, and start date help the scheduling team plan, and any specialties or preferences improve the match.

## Is the patient's information secure?

Yes. Referral details are handled as protected health information. Within StaffPick, therapists only see what they need to decide on a case — never the full patient record — until a case is assigned.

## Who do I contact for help?

Reach out to your First Class Therapy Solutions scheduling contact. Quoting a reference number always speeds things up.
