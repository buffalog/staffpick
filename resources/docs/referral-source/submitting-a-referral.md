# Submitting a Referral

Open your office's secure intake link and fill in the form. Here's what each section means and why it matters.

## Patient details

The patient's **first and last name** and **service address** (where care will be delivered). The address is important: StaffPick uses it to find therapists who are close enough to take the case, so enter it as accurately as you can. After entering the address you may be asked to confirm its location on a map.

## Discipline

The **therapy discipline** the patient needs — **Physical Therapy (PT)**, **Occupational Therapy (OT)**, or **Speech-Language Pathology (SLP)**. Only therapists of the discipline you choose will be matched, so pick the one the patient requires.

## Specialties

If the case calls for a particular focus (for example a paediatric, neurological, or hand-therapy specialty), select it. The specialty options update to match the discipline you chose. Specialties help us match a therapist with the right expertise, but they're optional — leave them blank if none apply.

## Preferences

Optional preferences that narrow who the case is offered to:

- **Provider gender preference** — if the patient has a preference, only therapists of that gender are offered the case.
- **Language** — note the patient's preferred language so we can favour a therapist who speaks it.

Only set a preference when it genuinely matters — each one narrows the pool of available therapists.

## Authorization & scheduling

Where applicable, include the **authorization number**, the number of **visits authorized**, the **frequency**, and the **start date**. These help the scheduling team plan the case correctly.

## Notes

Use the notes field for anything else the scheduling team should know that doesn't fit a field above.

## Submitting

When you submit, the form checks the required fields and gives you a **reference number** on the confirmation screen. Save it — see **[After You Submit](after-you-submit)**.
