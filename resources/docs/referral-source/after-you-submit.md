# After You Submit

Once you submit a referral, it's in FCTS's hands. Here's what happens and what to expect.

## Your reference number

Every referral gets a unique **reference number** in the form `R-XXXXXX` (for example `R-4F9A2C`), shown on the confirmation screen. **Save it.** It's the fastest way for FCTS to find your case if you call or email to follow up.

## What happens next

1. **Received** — your referral arrives in FCTS's system as a new case and the scheduling team is notified.
2. **Matching** — StaffPick ranks available therapists for the case by discipline, distance, language, and availability.
3. **Offered** — the best-fit therapists are offered the case in order. The first to accept is assigned.
4. **Assigned** — once a therapist accepts, FCTS coordinates scheduling and next steps with the patient.

Most cases move through these stages on their own; the scheduling team steps in where needed.

## What to expect from FCTS

- A **confirmation** that the referral was received (on screen, and via your office's channel where set up).
- Follow-up from the scheduling team as the case is placed, especially if they need anything more from you.
- If the case is in a thin-coverage area, placement can take a little longer while the search radius is widened — the team handles this for you.

## Following up

If you need a status update, contact FCTS and quote your **reference number**. That pulls up the exact case immediately.

## If something was wrong on the form

If you spot a mistake after submitting (wrong address, discipline, or detail), contact FCTS with the reference number so they can correct it before the case is placed.
